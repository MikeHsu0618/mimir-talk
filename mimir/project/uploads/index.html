Why it matters

Four reasons to instrument your LLM.

Model Improvement

Observability enables the collection of user interaction data, so you can continuously tune and improve your models for higher-quality results.

Issue Tracking

Effective logging and traceability make it possible to quickly pinpoint and resolve abnormal outputs — improving development efficiency.

Risk Management

Without observability it's difficult to detect inappropriate or risky outputs in time — leading to regulatory and brand risks.

Cost Optimization

Track resource usage and token consumption so you can control costs and optimize your infrastructure.
